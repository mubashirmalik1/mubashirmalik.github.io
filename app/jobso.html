Jobso

Project information

  • Category: Mobile App

Jobso is the application staffs Canadian Companies who are Hiring Employees long term or temporarily. Employees are required to download the Jobso mobile app to set up their Employee Account to apply for local job postings. Positions are posted by the Employer on the desktop platform or by a Jobso Account Manager on their behalf. Employers will then be shown applicants profiles as they apply and may offer the position to any qualified candidates. There is an option of video interview and live chat with candidates. Employer can also track the employees current location. and view the work daily work recode of each employee.
